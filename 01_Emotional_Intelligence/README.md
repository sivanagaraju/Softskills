# üß† Emotional Intelligence (EQ) Learning Path

Emotional Intelligence is the ability to recognize, understand, and manage our own emotions while also recognizing, understanding, and influencing the emotions of others. It serves as the "Operating System" for all other soft skills.

## üìÅ Sub-Folder Overview

### [01_Self_Awareness](./01_Self_Awareness)

**Objective:** The foundation of EQ. Understanding who you are.

- Recognizing your own emotions and their effects.
- Knowing your strengths and limits.
- Developing a strong sense of one's self-worth and capabilities.
- Identifying personal "triggers" that cause emotional reactions.

### [02_Self_Regulation](./02_Self_Regulation)

**Objective:** Managing your internal states and impulses.

- Keeping disruptive emotions and impulses in check.
- Maintaining standards of honesty and integrity.
- Taking responsibility for personal performance.
- Handling change with flexibility.

### [03_Intrinsic_Motivation](./03_Intrinsic_Motivation)

**Objective:** Emotional tendencies that guide or facilitate reaching goals.

- Striving to improve or meet a standard of excellence.
- Aligning with the goals of a group or organization.
- Readiness to act on opportunities.
- Persistence in pursuing goals despite obstacles and setbacks.

### [04_Empathy_and_Social_Awareness](./04_Empathy_and_Social_Awareness)

**Objective:** Awareness of others' feelings, needs, and concerns.

- Sensing others' feelings and perspectives.
- Taking an active interest in their concerns.
- Anticipating, recognizing, and meeting customer/client needs.
- Reading a group's emotional currents and power relationships.

### [05_Social_Skills_and_Relationship_Management](./05_Social_Skills_and_Relationship_Management)

**Objective:** Adeptness at inducing desirable responses in others.

- Wielding effective tactics for persuasion.
- Sending clear and convincing messages.
- Inspiring and guiding groups and people.
- Negotiating and resolving disagreements.
- Nurturing instrumental relationships.

### [06_Handling_Pressure_and_Stress](./06_Handling_Pressure_and_Stress)

**Objective:** Strategies for high-stakes environments.

- Psychological safety and resilience techniques.
- Decision-making under emotional duress.
- Burnout prevention and recovery.

### [07_Emotional_Intelligence_Frameworks](./07_Emotional_Intelligence_Frameworks)

**Objective:** Theoretical Knowledge.

- Studying models like Daniel Goleman's 5 Pillars.
- Understanding the neurobiology of emotions (e.g., the "Amygdala Hijack").
- EQ vs. IQ comparisons and research.
