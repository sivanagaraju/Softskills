# English for Client Communication

Talking to clients doesn't have to feel complicated or stressful. The way you communicate can make a big difference in how they see you and your work. A good conversation can build trust, clear up confusion, and keep everyone on the same page.

This guide is here to help you master those client conversations with easy, practical tips. For each situation, you'll see two responses:

- **Normal Response** — the usual, straightforward way
- **Effective Response** — a more thoughtful, polished version

The goal is to help you connect better, handle tricky moments gracefully, and leave your clients feeling confident in you.

## 1. Project Management & Progress

| Situation | Normal Response | Effective Response |
|-----------|----------------|-------------------|

| Providing Project Roadmap | "Here's our plan." | "We've developed a comprehensive project roadmap outlining each phase and key milestones. Let's review it together to ensure it aligns with your vision and timeline." |
| Managing Time Expectations | "This will take time." | "To set realistic expectations, let's outline the project timeline in detail. We'll highlight key milestones and ensure you're updated on our progress regularly." |
